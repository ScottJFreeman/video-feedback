Click to play

To move around, press Z or the button
  • W, A, S, D, R, F moves forwards, left, backwards, right, up, and down.
  • Hold shift to move faster.
  • Right click and drag to rotate your view.
  • Press U to reset if you get lost.
  • Press escape or Z to exit.

When you're not moving around, you can control the animation
  • Left-click and drag to move the pattern around (or use I, J, K, L).
  • Right-click and drag to rotate the pattern (or use A, D).
  • Scroll up to zoom in, scroll down to zoom out (or use W, S).
  • Resume the automatic animation by pressing X or the button.
